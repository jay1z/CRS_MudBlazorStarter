# Submitting a Reserve Study Request

This guide walks you through the process of requesting a new reserve study for your community.

---

## When to Request a Reserve Study

You may need a reserve study when:

- **First-time study** - Your community has never had a reserve study
- **Update required** - Your existing study is more than 3-5 years old
- **State requirement** - Many states require periodic reserve study updates
- **Major changes** - Significant property changes (new buildings, renovations, etc.)
- **Financing needs** - Lenders often require current reserve studies
- **Board request** - The HOA board wants updated financial projections

---

## Starting a New Request

### Accessing the Request Form

1. **Log in** to your portal
2. Navigate to **Reserve Studies** in the main menu
3. Click the **"Request New Study"** or **"New Request"** button

Alternatively, you may have received a direct link from your reserve study company.

---

## The Request Process

The request form uses a step-by-step wizard to collect all necessary information.

### Step 1: Community Information

Enter details about your community:

| Field | Description | Required |
|-------|-------------|----------|
| **Community Name** | The official name of your HOA or community | ✅ Yes |
| **Address** | Street address of the community | ✅ Yes |
| **City, State, ZIP** | Location details | ✅ Yes |
| **Property Type** | Condominium, townhome, single-family, mixed-use, etc. | ✅ Yes |
| **Number of Units** | Total residential units in the community | ✅ Yes |
| **Year Built** | When the community was originally constructed | ✅ Yes |

> **Tip**: If your community has multiple phases built in different years, enter the year of the oldest phase.

#### Additional Information (if applicable)

- **Number of Buildings** - How many structures are in the community
- **Total Square Footage** - Approximate size of all common areas
- **Previous Study Date** - When your last reserve study was completed

---

### Step 2: Contact Information

Provide contact details for the study:

| Field | Description | Required |
|-------|-------------|----------|
| **Primary Contact Name** | Person who will coordinate the study | ✅ Yes |
| **Email Address** | Where to send updates and documents | ✅ Yes |
| **Phone Number** | Best number to reach you | ✅ Yes |
| **Role** | Board president, property manager, treasurer, etc. | Optional |

#### Secondary Contact (Optional)

You can add a secondary contact who will also receive updates:

- Property manager (if primary contact is a board member)
- Board treasurer or other officer
- Management company representative

> **Note**: All contacts will receive important notifications about the study progress.

---

### Step 3: Components/Elements

Select the components (assets) to include in your reserve study:

#### Common Components

| Category | Examples |
|----------|----------|
| **Roofing** | Shingle roofs, flat roofs, tile roofs |
| **Exterior** | Siding, painting, stucco, balconies |
| **Paving** | Parking lots, driveways, sidewalks |
| **Recreation** | Pool, spa, tennis courts, playground |
| **Mechanical** | HVAC, elevators, pumps |
| **Landscaping** | Irrigation, trees, fencing |
| **Structural** | Foundations, retaining walls |

#### How to Select Components

1. Review the **pre-populated list** of common components
2. **Check the boxes** for components that exist in your community
3. **Uncheck** any components that don't apply
4. Use **"Add Custom Component"** for unique items not listed

> **Tip**: When in doubt, include a component. The specialist can remove it during the site visit if it doesn't apply.

---

### Step 4: Additional Information

Provide any extra details that will help with your study:

#### Optional Fields

- **Special Requests** - Any specific concerns or focus areas
- **Recent Major Repairs** - Work completed in the last 5 years
- **Known Issues** - Problems that need immediate attention
- **Budget Constraints** - If you have a target budget for contributions
- **Preferred Timeline** - When you need the study completed

#### Document Uploads (Optional)

You may upload supporting documents:

- Previous reserve study (PDF)
- Recent inspection reports
- Governing documents (CC&Rs)
- Site maps or plans
- Photos of specific concerns

---

### Step 5: Review and Submit

Before submitting, review all information:

1. **Verify community details** are accurate
2. **Confirm contact information** is correct
3. **Review selected components** 
4. **Read the terms and conditions**
5. **Check the acceptance box** to agree to terms
6. **Click "Submit"** to send your request

---

## After Submission

### Confirmation

After submitting, you'll see a confirmation screen with:

- ✅ **Success message** - Your request has been received
- 📧 **Email confirmation** - Check your inbox for details
- 🔗 **Tracking link** - Monitor your request status

### What Happens Next

| Step | Who | Timeline |
|------|-----|----------|
| **Request Received** | System | Immediate |
| **Request Reviewed** | Company Admin | 1-2 business days |
| **Request Approved** | Company Admin | After review |
| **Proposal Sent** | Specialist | 3-5 business days |
| **You Review Proposal** | You | At your convenience |

### Email Notifications

You'll receive emails at each stage:

1. **Request Submitted** - Confirmation with reference number
2. **Request Approved** - Your request has been accepted
3. **Proposal Ready** - A proposal is available for your review

| **Proposal Sent** | Proposal available for your review |

## Editing a Request

### Before Approval

If you need to make changes before your request is reviewed:

1. Go to **Reserve Studies** in your dashboard
2. Find your pending request
3. Click **"Edit"** to modify details
4. Save your changes

### After Approval

Once a request is approved, contact the specialist or company directly to make changes. Major scope changes may require an amendment.

---

## Canceling a Request

If you need to cancel a request:

1. Contact the reserve study company directly
2. Provide your request reference number
3. State the reason for cancellation

> **Note**: Once a proposal has been accepted and work has begun, cancellation may involve fees per the agreement terms.

---

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues

**Q: I can't submit the form.**
A: Ensure all required fields (marked with *) are completed. Check for validation errors highlighted in red.

**Q: I need to add components not on the list.**
A: Use the "Add Custom Component" option to add unique items specific to your community.

**Q: I submitted but didn't receive a confirmation email.**
A: Check your spam/junk folder. If not there, verify your email address was entered correctly and contact support.

**Q: How do I request a study for multiple communities?**
A: Submit a separate request for each community. Each community receives its own reserve study.
