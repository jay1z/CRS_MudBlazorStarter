# Providing Financial Information

After you accept a proposal, you'll be asked to provide financial information about your community. This data is essential for creating an accurate reserve study and funding plan.

---

## Why Financial Information is Needed

Your reserve study requires financial data to:

| Purpose | Why It Matters |
|---------|----------------|
| **Current Reserve Balance** | Starting point for funding projections |
| **Contribution History** | Understanding your funding patterns |
| **Planned Expenditures** | Accounting for upcoming projects |
| **Loan Information** | Factoring in existing obligations |
| **Interest Earnings** | Projecting investment returns |

> **Note**: Without accurate financial data, the funding plan cannot properly reflect your community's situation.

---

## Receiving the Request

### Email Notification

After your proposal is accepted, you'll receive an email:

- **Subject**: "Financial Information Request for [Community Name]"
- **Content**: Instructions and a link to the form
- **Deadline**: Suggested timeframe for submission

### In-App Notification

You'll also see notifications in your portal:

- **Dashboard** - "Action Required: Submit Financial Information"
- **Study Status** - Changes to "Financial Info Requested"
- **Notification Bell** - Alert with direct link

---

## Accessing the Form

### From Email

1. Click the **link in the email** to go directly to the form
2. Log in if prompted
3. The form opens automatically

### From Portal

1. **Log in** to your portal
2. Go to **Reserve Studies**
3. Find the study with status **"Financial Info Requested"**
4. Click **"Submit Financial Information"** or **"Provide Info"**

## Required Information

### Reserve Balance Information

| Field | Description | Example |
|-------|-------------|---------|
| **January 1st Reserve Balance** | Balance at start of current year | $150,000 |
| **December 31st Reserve Balance** | Expected year-end balance | $175,000 |

> **Tip**: Find these figures in your most recent financial statements or treasurer's report.

### Budgeted Reserve Contributions

Annual amounts your community allocates to reserves:

| Field | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Last Year's Contribution** | What you budgeted for reserves last year |
| **Current Year's Contribution** | This year's budgeted reserve amount |
| **Next Year's Contribution** | Planned amount for next year (if known) |

### Operating Budget

General community operating expenses:

| Field | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Current Year Operating Budget** | Total non-reserve expenses this year |
| **Next Year Operating Budget** | Projected expenses for next year |

### Community Information

| Field | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Total Number of Units** | Residential units in the community |
| **Annual Meeting Month** | When your HOA holds its annual meeting |

---

## Optional Information

### Loans and Financing

If your community has outstanding loans:

| Field | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Loan Amount** | Original loan principal |
| **Balance Remaining** | Current outstanding balance |
| **Expected Payoff Year** | When the loan will be paid off |

### Special Assessments

If you've levied special assessments:

| Field | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Assessment Amount** | Total special assessment |
| **Balance Remaining** | Amount still owed by owners |
| **Expected Collection Year** | When fully collected |

### Planned Projects

List any scheduled or committed projects:

| Information to Include | Example |
|-----------------------|---------|
| **Description** | Parking lot resurfacing |
| **Planned Year** | 2025 |
| **Quoted/Estimated Cost** | $85,000 |

### Insurance and Interest

| Field | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Property Insurance Deductible** | Your HOA's deductible amount |
| **Interest Rate on Reserve Funds** | Rate earned on reserve investments |

## Completing the Form

### Step-by-Step Process

1. **Gather your documents** before starting:
   - Current year budget
   - Prior year financial statements
   - Bank statements showing reserve balance
   - Loan documents (if applicable)
   - Board meeting minutes with planned projects

2. **Enter information** in each section
3. **Save your progress** if you need to pause
4. **Review all entries** for accuracy
5. **Submit** when complete

### Saving Progress

The form automatically saves as you type. You can also:

- Click **"Save Draft"** to explicitly save
- Close the browser and return later
- Your progress will be restored when you return

> **Tip**: Don't worry about completing everything in one session. Save your progress and return when you have all the information.

### Validation

The form validates your entries:

- **Required fields** are marked with an asterisk (*)
- **Format errors** appear as you type (e.g., invalid numbers)
- **Logical checks** flag inconsistencies (e.g., end balance lower than start with contributions)

## Submitting the Form

### Before You Submit

Review your entries:

- [ ] All required fields are completed
- [ ] Numbers are accurate (double-check balances)
- [ ] Documents are attached (if requested)
- [ ] Contact information is current

### Submitting

1. Click **"Submit Financial Information"**
2. Confirm your submission in the popup
3. You'll see a **success message**
4. An email confirmation is sent

### What Happens Next

| Timeline | Action |
|----------|--------|
| **Immediate** | Confirmation email sent |
| **1-3 days** | Specialist reviews your submission |
| **If questions** | You may be contacted for clarification |
| **When complete** | Status updates to "Financial Info Received" |

---

## After Submission

### Confirmation

You'll receive:

- ✅ On-screen confirmation message
- 📧 Email with submission summary
- 📋 Status update in your portal

### Review Process

The specialist will review your information:

1. **Verify completeness** - All needed data provided
2. **Check consistency** - Numbers make sense together
3. **Request clarification** - If anything is unclear
4. **Accept** - Mark as received and proceed

### If Changes Are Needed

If the specialist needs additional information:

- You'll receive an **email notification**
- Specific **questions or requests** will be listed
- You can **edit and resubmit** the form

## Tips for Accurate Submission

### Where to Find Information

| Information | Typical Source |
|-------------|----------------|
| Reserve Balance | Bank statement, treasurer report |
| Contributions | Annual budget document |
| Operating Budget | Annual budget document |
| Loan Details | Loan agreement, payment schedule |
| Unit Count | CC&Rs, property records |

### Common Mistakes to Avoid

| Mistake | Solution |
|---------|----------|
| Using estimated balances | Use actual bank statement figures |
| Mixing reserve and operating funds | Report only reserve fund amounts |
| Forgetting pending assessments | Include special assessments in progress |
| Outdated information | Use current year data |

### Getting Help

If you're unsure about any field:

- Look for **info icons** (ℹ️) next to fields for explanations
- **Contact your property manager** or treasurer
- **Reach out to the specialist** via email or phone
- Leave a **note** in the comments section explaining uncertainty

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: What if I don't have exact figures?**
A: Use your best estimates and note which figures are estimated. The specialist can work with reasonable approximations.

**Q: Can our property manager fill this out?**
A: Yes, anyone with access to the financial information can complete the form. Just ensure they have the login credentials.

**Q: What if our fiscal year isn't the calendar year?**
A: Note your fiscal year dates in the comments. Provide figures aligned with your fiscal year.

**Q: How long do I have to submit?**
A: Check the request email for the deadline. Typically 2-3 weeks, but timely submission keeps your study on track.

**Q: Is this information confidential?**
A: Yes, financial data is securely stored and only accessible to authorized personnel working on your study.
