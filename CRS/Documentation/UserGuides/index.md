# CRS User Guides

Welcome to the CRS (Community Reserve Study) documentation. Select the guide that matches your role to get started.

---

## Choose Your Guide

### 🏠 [HOA User Guide](./HOAUserGuide/index.md)
**For**: HOA board members, property managers, and community representatives

Learn how to:
- Submit reserve study requests
- Review and accept proposals
- Provide financial information
- Access your completed reports
- View and pay invoices

---

### 🔧 [Specialist Guide](./SpecialistGuide/index.md)
**For**: Reserve study specialists, inspectors, and analysts

Learn how to:
- Manage your assigned studies
- Create and send proposals
- Conduct site visits and upload data
- Generate reserve study reports
- Use the Kanban workflow board

---

### ⚙️ [Admin Guide](./AdminGuide/index.md)
**For**: Company administrators and tenant owners

Learn how to:
- Manage users and permissions
- Oversee the study workflow
- Configure system settings
- Handle invoicing and payments
- Review analytics and reports
