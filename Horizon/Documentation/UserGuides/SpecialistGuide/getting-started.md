# Getting Started

Welcome to the Specialist Guide! This section will help you get oriented with your dashboard, understand your role, and navigate the system efficiently.

---

## Your Role as a Specialist

As a **TenantSpecialist**, you are responsible for the core work of conducting reserve studies:

| Responsibility | Description |
|----------------|-------------|
| **Create Proposals** | Draft service agreements for HOA clients |
| **Collect Data** | Request and review financial information |
| **Conduct Site Visits** | Inspect community properties |
| **Assess Components** | Evaluate condition and remaining life |
| **Generate Reports** | Create comprehensive reserve study documents |
| **Quality Work** | Ensure accuracy before QA review |

### What You Can Do

| Action | Access |
|--------|--------|
| ✅ View assigned studies | Your workload |
| ✅ Create/edit proposals | For assigned studies |
| ✅ Request financial info | From HOA clients |
| ✅ Schedule site visits | Using the calendar |
| ✅ Upload photos/notes | From inspections |
| ✅ Edit components | Costs, life, condition |
| ✅ Generate reports | Draft and preview |
| ✅ Submit for QA | Send to admin for review |

### What You Cannot Do

| Action | Who Can |
|--------|---------|
| ❌ Approve requests | TenantOwner only |
| ❌ Assign specialists | TenantOwner only |
| ❌ Publish final reports | TenantOwner only |
| ❌ Manage users | TenantOwner only |
| ❌ Configure settings | TenantOwner only |

## Logging In

### Accessing the Portal

1. Navigate to your company's portal URL
2. Enter your **email address**
3. Enter your **password**
4. Click **"Log In"**

### First-Time Setup

If you're a new specialist:

1. You'll receive a **welcome email** with temporary credentials
2. Log in and **change your password**
3. Update your **profile information**
4. Set your **notification preferences**

### Password Reset

If you forget your password:

1. Click **"Forgot Password?"** on the login page
2. Enter your email address
3. Check your inbox for the reset link
4. Create a new password

## Understanding Your Assigned Studies

Studies are assigned to you by the TenantOwner (admin).

### How Assignment Works

1. **HOA submits request** → Admin reviews
2. **Admin approves** → Request enters queue
3. **Admin assigns you** → Study appears in your list
4. **You take over** → Work through the workflow

### Viewing Your Studies

1. Click **"Reserve Studies"** in the navigation
2. Or click **"My Work"** under Specialist Tools
3. Your assigned studies are highlighted

### Study Status Flow

As a specialist, you'll work through these statuses:

```
Assigned → ProposalCreated → ProposalSent → ProposalAccepted
    ↓
FinancialInfoRequested → FinancialInfoReceived
    ↓
SiteVisitScheduled → SiteVisitCompleted
    ↓
FundingPlanReady → NarrativeInProcess → ReportDrafted
    ↓
(Submit for QA) → Admin Review → Published
```

## Notification Preferences

Stay informed without being overwhelmed.

### Types of Notifications

| Notification | When Sent |
|--------------|-----------|
| **Study Assigned** | New study assigned to you |
| **Proposal Accepted** | Client accepted your proposal |
| **Financial Info Submitted** | Client submitted data |
| **Site Visit Reminder** | Upcoming inspection |
| **QA Feedback** | Admin reviewed your report |
| **Message Received** | New message from client/team |

### Configuring Notifications

1. Click your **profile icon** (top right)
2. Select **Settings** or **Preferences**
3. Navigate to **Notifications**
4. Toggle options:
   - **Email notifications** - Receive emails
   - **In-app notifications** - See in portal
   - **Daily digest** - Summary vs. immediate

### Recommended Settings

For specialists, we recommend:

| Setting | Recommendation |
|---------|----------------|
| Study assigned | ✅ Immediate |
| Proposal accepted | ✅ Immediate |
| Financial info received | ✅ Immediate |
| Site visit reminders | ✅ Immediate + email |
| QA feedback | ✅ Immediate |
| Daily summary | ✅ End of day |

## Mobile Considerations

If you need to access the portal from a mobile device:

### Browser Access

- The portal is **responsive** and works on mobile browsers
- Best experience on **tablets** for data entry
- Use **landscape mode** for better navigation

### During Site Visits

For field work:

- **Take photos** with your phone camera
- **Upload photos** when back on WiFi
- **Take notes** in the app or a notepad
- Ensure **offline access** to community details (print or screenshot)

### Tips for Mobile Use

- **Pre-download** study details before site visits
- Use **photo naming** conventions for organization
- **Save drafts** frequently when on unstable connections
- **Upload photos** in batches when on stable WiFi

## Getting Help

### In-App Resources

- Look for **info icons** (ℹ️) for field explanations
- Check **tooltips** on buttons and fields
- Use the **help section** if available

### Team Support

- **Message** your admin through the portal
- Ask questions via **team chat** (if available)
- Contact **support** for technical issues

### Common Questions

**Q: I don't see a study that was assigned to me.**
A: Refresh the page. Check filters aren't hiding it. Contact your admin if it's still missing.

**Q: How do I hand off a study to another specialist?**
A: Only admins can reassign studies. Contact your TenantOwner.

**Q: Can I work on studies not assigned to me?**
A: Typically no. Studies are assigned for accountability. Ask your admin if you need to assist.
