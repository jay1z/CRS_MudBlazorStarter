# Invoices and Payments

This guide explains how to view your invoices and make payments through the portal.

---

## Understanding Invoices

### When Invoices Are Sent

Invoices are generated at different milestones depending on your payment schedule:

| Milestone | When | Typical Amount |
|-----------|------|----------------|
| **Deposit** | After proposal acceptance | 25-50% of total |
| **Site Visit Complete** | After inspection | 25-33% of total |
| **Draft Report** | When draft is delivered | 25% of total |
| **Final Delivery** | Upon completion | Remaining balance |
| **Full Payment** | Single invoice option | 100% of total |

Your specific payment schedule is outlined in your accepted proposal.

### Invoice Contents

Each invoice includes:

| Element | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **Invoice Number** | Unique reference (e.g., INV-2024-0123) |
| **Invoice Date** | When the invoice was created |
| **Due Date** | Payment deadline |
| **Community Name** | Your HOA/community |
| **Description** | Services being billed |
| **Amount** | Total due |
| **Payment Status** | Current status |

---

## Invoice Status

Invoices go through several statuses:

| Status | Meaning | Action Needed |
|--------|---------|---------------|
| **Draft** | Not yet sent to you | None - internal preparation |
| **Sent** | Delivered, awaiting payment | Payment required |
| **Paid** | Payment received in full | None |
| **Partially Paid** | Some payment received | Pay remaining balance |
| **Overdue** | Past the due date | Pay immediately |
| **Voided** | Cancelled | None |

## Making a Payment

### Online Payment

The easiest way to pay is through the portal:

1. **Find your invoice** in the Invoices section
2. Click **"Pay Now"** or **"Make Payment"**
3. Enter payment details:
   - **Card Number** - Your credit or debit card
   - **Expiration Date** - MM/YY format
   - **CVV** - Security code on back of card
   - **Billing ZIP** - ZIP code for the card
4. Review the **amount** being charged
5. Click **"Submit Payment"** or **"Pay $X,XXX.XX"**

## Payment Issues

### Payment Declined

If your payment is declined:

| Reason | Solution |
|--------|----------|
| **Insufficient funds** | Use a different card or add funds |
| **Card expired** | Use a valid card |
| **Incorrect details** | Double-check card number and CVV |
| **Bank block** | Contact your bank to authorize |
| **Daily limit** | Try a smaller amount or different card |

### Duplicate Payment

If you accidentally paid twice:

1. **Don't panic** - Contact the company immediately
2. Provide both **transaction IDs**
3. A **refund** will be processed
4. Refunds typically take **5-10 business days**

### Payment Not Reflecting

If your payment isn't showing:

1. Check your **email** for confirmation
2. Verify the charge on your **bank statement**
3. Wait **24 hours** for system updates
4. Contact support with your transaction details

## Late Payments

### Overdue Invoices

Invoices become overdue after the due date:

- **Visual indicator** - Status changes to "Overdue"
- **Dashboard alert** - Warning displayed
- **Email reminders** - Automatic notifications sent

### Late Fees

Depending on the company's policy:

| Policy | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **Grace Period** | Some companies allow extra days |
| **Late Fee** | Percentage or flat fee added |
| **Interest** | Monthly interest on balance |

> **Important**: Check your proposal for specific late payment terms.

### Avoiding Late Fees

- Set up **calendar reminders** for due dates
- Pay **immediately** upon receiving invoices
- Contact the company **before** the due date if you need an extension

## Alternative Payment Methods

### Check Payments

If you prefer to pay by check:

1. **Contact the company** for mailing address
2. Make check payable to the **company name**
3. Include **invoice number** on the check
4. Mail with sufficient time before due date

### Bank Transfer / ACH

For wire or ACH payments:

1. **Request banking details** from the company
2. Set up the transfer through your bank
3. Include **invoice number** in the reference
4. Allow **2-3 business days** for processing

### Payment Plans

If you need extended terms:

1. **Contact the company** before the due date
2. Explain your situation
3. Request a **payment plan** if available
4. Get any agreement in writing

## Credits and Adjustments

### Credit Memos

If you receive a credit:

- Shows as a **negative amount** or separate credit memo
- Can be **applied** to future invoices
- May result from overpayment or price adjustment

### Refunds

Refunds are issued when:

- Overpayment occurred
- Service was cancelled (per terms)
- Billing error was made

Refunds typically return to the **original payment method**.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Can I pay with a credit card?**
A: Yes, Visa, Mastercard, American Express, and Discover are accepted.

**Q: Is online payment secure?**
A: Yes, payments are processed through Stripe with bank-level encryption. We never store your full card details.

**Q: What if I can't pay by the due date?**
A: Contact the reserve study company before the due date to discuss options.

**Q: How do I get a receipt for my records?**
A: Receipts are emailed automatically and available in your Documents section.

**Q: Can the HOA pay from our reserve account?**
A: Consult your HOA's policies. Many communities pay reserve study costs from operating funds, not reserves.

**Q: Can I dispute a charge?**
A: Contact the company first to resolve any billing questions before disputing with your bank.
