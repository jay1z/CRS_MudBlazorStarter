# Getting Started

Welcome to your reserve study portal! This guide will help you access your account, understand your dashboard, and navigate the system.

---

## Accessing Your Portal

### Logging In

1. **Open your browser** and navigate to the portal URL provided by your reserve study company
2. **Enter your email address** in the email field
3. **Enter your password** in the password field
4. **Click "Log In"** to access your portal

> **Tip**: Bookmark the login page for quick access in the future.

### First-Time Login

If this is your first time logging in:

1. You should have received a **welcome email** with your login credentials
2. Click the link in the email or navigate to the portal URL
3. Enter the **temporary password** provided
4. You'll be prompted to **create a new password**
5. Choose a strong password with at least 8 characters

### Forgot Your Password?

1. Click the **"Forgot Password?"** link on the login page
2. Enter your **email address**
3. Check your inbox for a **password reset email**
4. Click the reset link and **create a new password**
5. Return to the login page and sign in

> **Note**: Password reset links expire after 24 hours. If your link has expired, request a new one.

## Notifications

Stay informed about your reserve studies:

### Types of Notifications

- **Proposal Ready** - A new proposal is available for your review
- **Information Requested** - The specialist needs financial data from you
- **Site Visit Scheduled** - An inspection has been scheduled
- **Report Ready** - Your completed reserve study is available
- **Invoice Sent** - A new invoice requires payment

### Notification Settings

1. Click your **profile icon** in the top right
2. Select **Settings** or **Preferences**
3. Choose your **notification preferences**:
   - Email notifications
   - In-app notifications
   - Notification frequency

### In-App Help

### Contact Support

If you need assistance:

1. **Email** - Contact the reserve study company directly (shown in your proposal)
2. **Phone** - Call the number listed on your invoice or proposal
3. **In-App** - Use the support ticket feature if available

### Common Questions

**Q: I can't see my reserve study.**
A: Make sure you're logged in with the correct email address. Contact your reserve study company if the issue persists.

**Q: My password isn't working.**
A: Use the "Forgot Password?" link to reset it. Check your spam folder for the reset email.

**Q: I need to add another community.**
A: Contact your reserve study company to request a new study for an additional community.
