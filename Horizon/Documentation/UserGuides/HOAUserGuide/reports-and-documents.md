# Reports and Documents

Throughout the reserve study process, various documents will be generated and shared with you. This guide explains how to access, understand, and download your documents.

---

## Document Types

Your portal contains several types of documents:

| Document Type | Description | When Available |
|---------------|-------------|----------------|
| **Proposals** | Service agreements for your review | After request approval |
| **Signed Proposals** | Accepted proposals with e-signature | After you accept |
| **Financial Info Confirmations** | Record of submitted financial data | After you submit |
| **Site Visit Notifications** | Scheduled inspection details | When visit is scheduled |
| **Draft Reports** | Preliminary reserve study (if included) | During review phase |
| **Final Reserve Study** | Completed study document | When study is complete |
| **Invoices** | Payment requests | At billing milestones |
| **Receipts** | Payment confirmations | After payment |

## Accessing Your Documents

### From the Dashboard

1. **Log in** to your portal
2. Look for the **"Recent Documents"** section on your dashboard
3. Click on any document to view or download

### From Reserve Studies

1. Go to **Reserve Studies** in the main menu
2. Click on the specific study
3. Select the **"Documents"** tab
4. Browse all documents for that study

### From the Documents Menu

1. Click **"Documents"** in the main navigation
2. See all documents across all your studies
3. Use filters to find specific document types

## Understanding Your Reserve Study Report

Your completed reserve study is the most important document. Here's what's included:

### Executive Summary

The first section provides a high-level overview:

| Element | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **Current Reserve Balance** | Starting point for projections |
| **Recommended Contribution** | Suggested annual funding amount |
| **Percent Funded** | How well-funded your reserves are |
| **Key Findings** | Important observations |

### Component Inventory

A detailed list of all major components:

| Information | Example |
|-------------|---------|
| **Component Name** | Asphalt Shingle Roof - Building A |
| **Category** | Roofing |
| **Useful Life** | 25 years |
| **Remaining Life** | 12 years |
| **Current Cost** | $85,000 |
| **Future Cost** | $112,000 (adjusted for inflation) |

### Condition Assessment

For each component:

- **Current Condition** - Excellent, Good, Fair, Poor
- **Observations** - Notes from the site inspection
- **Photos** - Visual documentation (if included)
- **Recommendations** - Maintenance or repair suggestions

### Funding Plan

The financial strategy for your reserves:

| Scenario | Description |
|----------|-------------|
| **Current Funding** | What happens if you continue current contributions |
| **Full Funding** | Contributions needed to maintain 100% funded |
| **Threshold Funding** | Minimum funding to avoid special assessments |
| **Statutory Funding** | Meeting state-required minimums (if applicable) |

### 30-Year Projection

A year-by-year forecast showing:

- **Contributions** - Money coming into reserves
- **Expenditures** - Projected replacement costs
- **Balance** - End-of-year reserve balance
- **Percent Funded** - Funding level over time

### Appendices

Additional supporting information:

- Detailed component tables
- Calculation methodology
- Assumptions used
- Glossary of terms

## Sharing Documents

### With Board Members

To share documents with your board:

1. **Download** the document
2. **Email** or share via your preferred method
3. Or grant them **portal access** (contact your company)

### With Property Managers

If your management company needs access:

- They may already have their own login
- Request additional user access through the company
- Or share downloaded copies directly

### Printing

To print documents:

1. **Download** the PDF
2. Open in your PDF reader
3. Select **Print**
4. Choose print settings (double-sided recommended for reports)

> **Tip**: Reserve studies can be 50-100+ pages. Consider printing only the executive summary for board meetings.

## Troubleshooting

### Document Won't Open

| Issue | Solution |
|-------|----------|
| PDF won't display | Download and open in Adobe Reader |
| Download fails | Try a different browser |
| File corrupted | Contact support for a new copy |

### Can't Find a Document

1. Check all studies (document might be in a different study)
2. Use the search function if available
3. Check your email for the original notification
4. Contact the company to request the document

### Access Denied

If you can't access a document:

- Verify you're logged in with the correct account
- The document may be for a different community
- Contact support to verify your permissions

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Can I get a printed copy mailed to me?**
A: Contact the reserve study company. Some offer printed copies for an additional fee.

**Q: How do I share the report with homeowners?**
A: Download the PDF and distribute according to your HOA's communication policies. Many communities post summaries in newsletters.

**Q: The figures in the report don't match what I submitted.**
A: The specialist may have made adjustments based on their analysis. Contact them for clarification.

**Q: Can I request changes to the report?**
A: Contact the specialist. Minor corrections are typically accommodated; significant changes may require an amendment.
