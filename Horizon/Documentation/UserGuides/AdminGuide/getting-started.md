## Logging In

### Accessing the Portal

1. Navigate to your company's portal URL
2. Enter your **email address**
3. Enter your **password**
4. Click **"Log In"**

### First-Time Setup

When your tenant is first created:

1. You receive a **welcome email** with login credentials
2. Log in and **change your password**
3. Complete your **company profile**
4. Configure **initial settings**
5. **Invite staff** users

## Multi-Tenant Context

### Understanding Tenants

Your organization is a **tenant** in the SaaS platform:

| Concept | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **Your Tenant** | Your company's isolated workspace |
| **Your Data** | Completely separate from others |
| **Your Users** | Only your staff and clients |
| **Your Settings** | Customized for your business |

### Tenant Isolation

- You **cannot** see other companies' data
- Other companies **cannot** see yours
- Platform admins may have **support access** (if enabled)
